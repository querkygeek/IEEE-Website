About PhD Summer School 2026

Theme: Next-Generation Electric Vehicle Charging Systems Using Wide Bandgap Power Devices

Sponsored by: IEEE Power Electronics Society (PELS)

The Ph.D. Summer School 2026 focuses on next-generation electric vehicle charging systems using Wide Bandgap (SiC/GaN) devices. The program is designed to provide advanced insights into converter design, control strategies, fast charging, and grid integration.

Participants will benefit from:

  • Expert Lectures: Talks by renowned national and international experts on hardware and software design of advanced power converter controlsystems.
  • Practical Sessions: Practical training on real-time charging system implementation.
  • Research Presentations and Interaction: 20-minute research presentation sessions, followed by 10 minutes of Q&A.
  • Recognition & Awards: Certificates for all participants and award to outstanding performers based on evaluation.
This Summer School blends knowledge transfer, practical experimentation, and research exposure, equipping participants with the skills to tackle challenges in sustainable mobility and cutting-edge power electronics applications.

About NIT Agartala

National Institute of Technology Agartala (NITA) is a premier institute of national importance, committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation. Equipped with modern facilities and a vibrant academic environment, NITA fosters technical expertise, interdisciplinary learning, and holistic development, serving as a key hub for engineering advancement in Northeast India.

About Department of Electrical Engineering, NITA

The Department of Electrical Engineering at NIT Agartala offers high-quality education and advanced research opportunities across core and emerging areas of electrical engineering. With a dedicated team of experienced faculty and motivated students, the department fosters innovation, technical excellence, and interdisciplinary learning. It is supported by well-equipped laboratories, including state-of-the-art Power Electronics and Drives facilities, enabling hands-on training, experimentation, and cutting-edge research.
